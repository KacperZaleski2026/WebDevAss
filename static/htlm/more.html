NO MORE COVID 2024

After Pandemic Opening Party

About the Festival

The pandemic brought unprecedented challenges, especially for the live entertainment industry and all of us who missed out on the joy of live events. But now, with restrictions lifted, the excitement is back, and the live entertainment industry is ready to make a triumphant return!

Mark your calendars for 20th June 2024 and get ready to be part of an unforgettable celebration! The NO MORE COVID 2024 festival promises to be a spectacular event filled with incredible live music, dazzling performances, and endless entertainment. Whether you're a music lover, a thrill-seeker, or just looking for a fun day out, this festival has something special for everyone. Don't miss out on the comeback party of the year!

Festival Details

  • Date: 20 June 2024
  • Stages: The festival will feature two stages, each hosting a variety of performances throughout the day.
  • Fireworks Show: At midnight, enjoy a breathtaking 20-minute fireworks display to light up the night sky.
  • Artists and Attractions: A wide array of artists will be performing, with numerous shopping and entertainment attractions available.
  • Famous Guests: Look out for appearances by well-known personalities and celebrities.
  • Food and Drink: Numerous drink and snack bars will be situated around each stage, offering a variety of refreshments.
